The best movies according to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

Directed by Frank Darabont, this drama is based on a Stephen King novella and tells the poignant story of hope, friendship, and redemption in the face of adversity.

The Godfather

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this classic crime film follows the powerful Corleone family as they navigate the world of organized crime in post-World War II America.

Inception

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this mind-bending science fiction thriller stars Leonardo DiCaprio and explores the realm of dreams and subconscious manipulation in a visually stunning and thought-provoking manner.
